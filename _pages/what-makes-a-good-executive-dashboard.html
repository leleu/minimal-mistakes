The Right Questions

Great reports start with asking the right questions. Start by writing down the types of decisions are necessary to run your business. Some common questions:

  • How much money am I making each month?
  • How am I doing compared to projections?
  • Am I spending to budget in my marketing spend?
  • What is my customer retention rate?
  • How happy are my customers?
  • How is my Sales team doing?

De-Silo Data

  • Strategic decisions require a holistic view
  • A quantitative 50k foot view of your business should include sales, marketing, customer service, and projections.
  • Avoid Vendor lock-in when you own your data

Automate Analysis

  • Exporting csv or Excel files is time consuming and error prone
  • Fine for learning what reports you use, but require a lot of effort for each new report
  • Analysts can spend more time exploring the data, less time loading it
